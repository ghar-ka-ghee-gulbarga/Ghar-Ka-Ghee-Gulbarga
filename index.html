üí° More Info ‚Äî Benefits, Uses & Tips

Health Benefits

Ghee is rich in fat-soluble vitamins A, D, E and K. It supports digestion, boosts immunity, and is used in Ayurveda for nourishment and healing.

Cooking Tips

Use cow ghee for light aroma and daily cooking. Use buffalo ghee for sweets and rich gravies. Add a spoon to dal or rice for enhanced flavor.

Storage & Shelf Life

Store in a cool, dry place. Ghee is shelf-stable; keep the jar sealed and use a clean spoon to avoid contamination.
